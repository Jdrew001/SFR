Thank You!

NTXD Spiritual First Responders

I would like to take this opportunity to say a big THANK YOU to all of our SFR volunteers and especially, to those who were able to be a part of the impact event June 5th - 7th at New Flame Church in Burleson!

Thank you for putting your collective burden into action by coming alongside New Flame Church to Bless The Community of Burleson and helping to grow the Kingdom of God here in North Texas!

Volunteers went out to Connect with the Community! Teams went out Saturday morning to invite individuals and families to join us at Warren Park for a "Bless The Community" Event.

And the Community of Burleson came out!

98 First Time Guests
100+ SFR Volunteers
12 Churches

Prayers Answered!

We prayed Friday night and Saturday for the rain to go around Burleson until after the Community Impact Event and the Lord answered! We had beautiful weather for the Event and after everything was put away, there was a deluge of rain. Thank you everyone who prayed for the event. Many of the first time guests and their children came for Prayer... And God Moved!

There were also guests who came for the Sunday Service at New Flame!

Special Thanks!

A special Thanks to Pastor Chase Medders and Evangelist Tanner Friend, the Children's puppet ministry team from New Life Apostolic Church in Cedar Hill. We also recognize the contribution of Richard & Stephanie Ramirez and Word on the Street for serving Street Tacos and giving out Bibles. Thanks also to Sharetha Wheeler, who led us in focused Prayer Friday evening. Thanks to our SFR Media Team and to the many volunteers from across North Texas whose contribution made this event so special.
