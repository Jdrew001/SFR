Thank You NTXD Spiritual First Responders!

We'd like to take this opportunity to say a big THANK YOU to all of our SFR volunteers and especially, to those who were able to be a part of the impact event last weekend at Solid Rock Church in Irving!

Thank you for putting your collective burden into action by coming alongside Solid Rock Church to Bless The Community of Irving and helping to grow the Kingdom of God here in North Texas!

There were approximately 35 first time registered guests at the Community Impact Event at 121 South Story Road on Saturday with more than 60 SFR Volunteers from 14 North Texas Churches.

Praise Report!

We saw 2 Baptized in Jesus Name! SFR volunteers prayed with Men, Women and Young People who were moved by the Spirit of the Lord.

Additionally, there were 7 first time guests in service on Sunday and Bible Studies have already started!

Special Thanks To:

• Pastors Ed Snyder & Frank Chavira — Preaching at this Bilingual Event
• Dallas First Church Children's Ministry Team — Ministering to the Children in attendance
• Luke Wheeler of The Life Church–Dallas & the SRC Praise Team (Host Church) — Music Ministry
• Richard & Stephanie Ramirez of New Life Church — Garland & Word on the Street — Street Tacos and Bibles
• Charlita Cloman of New Life Church–Garland — Leading focused Prayer Friday evening
• Kelli Repasz of Calvary Church — Denton & the SFR Media Team — Media Ministry
• All volunteers from across North Texas whose contribution made this event so special

THANK YOU NORTH TEXAS!
